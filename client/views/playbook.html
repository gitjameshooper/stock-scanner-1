Play Book

Failed Follow Through Momo

Time of Day: Morning
Type of Trade: Short (Day)
Description: Stock has trend in pre-market. Stock has gapped up from yesterday's close. It runs and over extends. Look for first bearish candle. Risking off the HOD.
Test to use: Investors live Test

Morning Flag

Time of Day: Morning
Type of Trade: Buy (Day)
Description: Stock opens with a morning push. It pullsback to VWAP. Look to buy around VWAP and eye other levels of support.
Test to use: Flag Test

Red to Green

Time of Day: Morning
Type of Trade: Buy (Day)
Description: Prior day stock has lots of volume. Look for pullback and support lines to join trend. Support was above $25 on this stock.
Test to use: Investors live Test

ABCD

Time of Day: Midday
Type of Trade: Buy (Day)
Description: Stock develops ABCD pattern. (A)Push in the morning (B)Pullback to VWAP (C)Consildates over VWAP (D)Breakout EOD Look to buy after pop over VWAP. Risk below (B) support
Test to use: ABCD Test

Speed

Time of Day: Anytime
Type of Trade: Buy/Short (Day)
Description: Stock runs and over extends quickly. Jump on trend off the bounce off VWAP. Usually best for scalping $.50 - $1 moves
Test to use: Speed Test

VWAP

Time of Day: Late Day
Type of Trade: Short (Day)
Description: Stock has trended all day. It over extends on VWAP +10% and it extends off trend line. Look for people taking profits in the last hr.
Test to use: VWAP Test

2 Large Candle Reversal

Time of Day: Multi-Day
Type of Trade: Buy/Short (Swing)
Description: Stock had twin tower type candles that are very large. Usually signals a swing trade reversal. Notice the stock has not had a considerable pullback yet. Look to buy/short the next day off red to green or green to red kind of move.
Test to use: Swing Test
