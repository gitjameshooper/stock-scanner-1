Play Book

Gap Test

Time of Day: Morning
Type of Trade: Short
Stock has trend in pre-market. Stock has gapped up from yesterday's close. It runs and over extends. Look for first bearish candle. Risking off the HOD.

ABCD Test

Time of Day: Midday
Type of Trade: Buy
Stock develops ABCD pattern. (A)Push in the morning (B)Pullback to VWAP (C)Consildates over VWAP (D)Breakout EOD Look to buy after pop over VWAP. Risk below (B) support

Speed Test

Time of Day: Anytime
Type of Trade: Buy/Short
Stock runs and over extends quickly. Jump on trend off the bounce off VWAP. Usually best for scalping $.50 - $1 moves

Swing Test

Time of Day: Multi-Day
Type of Trade: Buy/Short
Stock had twin tower type candles that are very large. Usually signals a swing trade reversal. Look to buy/short the next day off red to green or green to red kind of move.

VWAP Test

Time of Day: Late Day
Type of Trade: Short
Stock has trended all day. It over extends on VWAP +10% and it extends off trend line. Look for people taking profits in the last hr.

Morning Flag Test

Time of Day: Morning
Type of Trade: Buy
Stock opens with a morning push. It pullsback to VWAP. Look to buy around VWAP and eye other levels of support.

Red to Green Test

Time of Day: Morning
Type of Trade: Buy
Prior day stock has lots of volume. Look for pullback and support lines to join trend.
